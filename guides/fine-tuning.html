Fine-Tuning

Model customization with LoRA, QLoRA, and full fine-tuning.

Full Fine-Tuning

Updates all model weights — requires lots of compute and data.

LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)

Injects trainable adapters into frozen models — efficient and modular.

QLoRA

Quantized LoRA — enables fine-tuning large models on consumer hardware.
